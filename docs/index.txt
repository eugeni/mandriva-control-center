Getting help
============

Having trouble? We'd like to help!

* Try the :doc:`FAQ <faq/index>` -- it's got answers to many common questions.

* Looking for specific information? Try the :ref:`genindex` or:ref:`modindex`.

* Search for information in the `archives of the mandriva-cooker mailing list`_, or
  `post a question`_.

* Ask a question in the `#mcc2 IRC channel`_, or search the `IRC logs`_ to see
  if it's been asked before.

* Report bugs with Mcc2 in our `ticket tracker`_.

.. _archives of the mandriva-cooker mailing list: http://www.mandriva.com/
.. _post a question: http://forun.mandriva.com/
.. _#mcc2 IRC channel: irc://irc.freenode.net/mcc2
.. _IRC logs: http://www.mandriva.com/
.. _ticket tracker: http://qa.mandriva.com/
